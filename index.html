Tournament Overview

The Group Phase.

Each player plays every player in their group once. Three tournament points are awarded for a win, and zero tournament points for a loss. The four players with the highest tournament points from each group** will progress into the knockout phase of the tournament.

** Total points scored in each match are also added together, in the event of a tie-breaker.

The Knockout Phase.

Players will play each other once, in a best of fifteen match. The loser of the match will be illiminated, and the winner will progress to the next round. The two losing players from the semi-final, will play each other one last time for third place.
